# Tensors

tensor $\simeq$ multi-dimensional array

  - 0-dim tensor $\simeq$ scalar
  - 1-dim tensor $\simeq$ vector
  - 2-dim tensor $\simeq$ matrix

$$
1.0
$$

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
    1 & 2 & 3
\end{bmatrix}
\mbox{ or }
\begin{bmatrix}
    1 \\
    2 \\
    3
\end{bmatrix}
$$

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
    \begin{bmatrix}
        1 & 2 & 3
    \end{bmatrix} \\ 
        \begin{bmatrix}
        1 & 2 & 3
    \end{bmatrix} \\
        \begin{bmatrix}
        1 & 2 & 3
    \end{bmatrix}
\end{bmatrix}
